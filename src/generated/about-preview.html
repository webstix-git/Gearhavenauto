Our Story

The shop we'd want our own families to visit.

We started Gear Haven with a simple mission: create the kind of repair shop we'd want our own families to visit. Too many people have experienced confusing estimates, poor communication, and pressure to approve repairs they don't fully understand.

We wanted to build something different — a place where customers feel comfortable asking questions, receive honest recommendations, and know their vehicle is being cared for by people who genuinely have their best interests in mind.

Today, that mission continues with every vehicle that enters our shop.

Our Why

We believe trust is earned.

That's why we focus on clear communication, quality workmanship, and doing what's right for our customers — even when it's not the easiest option.

Whether you're in for an oil change, a major repair, or a complex diesel diagnostic, our goal is to provide the same level of care and attention we'd want for our own vehicles.
